Vision & Mission of ChE Department

VISION: The vision of the Department of Chemical Engineering, Heritage Institute of Technology, is to be a nationally recognized centre of excellence for studies in chemical engineering program coupled with research strengths in separation processes, biochemical engineering, modelling and simulation, chemical reaction engineering and process safety.
MISSION: The mission of the Department of Chemical Engineering at Heritage Institute of Technology is to educate students to become highly qualified chemical engineers, conduct innovative research in chemical engineering and related interdisciplinary areas and provide service to the profession and to society.
